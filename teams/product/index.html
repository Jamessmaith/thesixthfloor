Get team
feedback

With Overflow comments

  • Upload your presentation to the Overflow Cloud to get a share link that you can send to your peers. Installing the Overflow app is not essential for recipients to access the link.
  • Your team can view your design presentation on any popular device, such as desktops, phones, and tablets.
  • Everyone with the link can exchange feedback through Overflow comments, leading to faster design iteration and decision-making.

In popular collaboration tools

Embed your Overflow presentation in Trello, JIRA, Confluence, or any other tool your team uses to collaborate, in order to align and receive feedback.

Enhance teamwork

  • Overflow documents can be stored in private folders, or shared with all or just some stakeholders, depending on your team’s structure and needs.
  • Team members can download the underlying documents of all share links they are given access to.
